I realize now the yellow headlines were a bad choice.

Client & Patient Educators Association

The tricky bit here was to make a site with not much content or imagery seem exciting. Or, at least not boring.

The homepage video gives some movement and disguises an undefined brand identity. Bright colors and interesting imagery on the programming touts try to enliven arcane, technical programming.

The profile pictures were wildly different, so we went black and white for some consistency and authority. This site won't win any awards, but it's a good example of making the best of what you have.
